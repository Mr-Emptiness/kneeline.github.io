From Generation to Generation

It all began with a simple observation. My wife looked at my knees and said, “Look, each knee has a face.”

From that moment, the idea was born – to capture each person's knees as invisible storytellers. Every photo tells a story – of our origin, our movement, our being.

This is not just art – it's legacy. Knees are the spiritual axis, grounding us and carrying us forward.

Each NFT in this collection holds a value formula based on π – infinite and immeasurable, just like human experience itself.

No one truly knows where we come from – yet we are all part of something greater. One story, without end or beginning – it simply is.
